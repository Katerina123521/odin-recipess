Greek Salad

Description

A fresh and colorful salad with tomatoes, cucumber, olives, onion, and feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil, oregano, salt

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Combine in a bowl with olives and feta.
  3. Dress with olive oil and oregano, season to taste.
