Pastitsio

Description

Pastitsio is a classic Greek baked pasta dish with pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel topping.

Ingredients

  • 500 g pasta
  • 500 g ground beef
  • Tomatoes, onion, garlic
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Cheese, olive oil, spices

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Make the béchamel sauce.
  4. Layer pasta, sauce, and béchamel in dish.
  5. Bake until golden.
