Carrot Cake

Description

Carrot cake is a moist and flavorful cake made with grated carrots, warm spices, and topped with creamy frosting. Perfect for dessert or afternoon tea!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • Cream cheese frosting (for topping)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs, sugar, brown sugar, and oil until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients together. Fold in grated carrots and walnuts.
  5. Pour batter into pan and bake for 40–45 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool, then top with cream cheese frosting.
